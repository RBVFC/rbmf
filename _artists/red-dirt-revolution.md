Red Dirt Revolution formed in 2020, bringing together seasoned musicians from Baltimore’s vibrant music scene. Their unique sound combines dual guitars and harmonious vocals, captivating audiences at local venues. 
In 2021, the band released their debut EP, showcasing original songs that reflect their love for country music. The EP received positive reviews and increased their fanbase significantly.
By 2022, Red Dirt Revolution was performing at major music festivals across the region, earning a reputation for their high-energy shows and engaging stage presence.
Looking ahead, the band aims to tour nationally and release a full-length album, continuing to share their passion for country music with fans everywhere.
Featuring Chad Boston (Bass), Glenn Schloer (Guitar), Phil Lathroum (Lead Vocal/Acoustic Guitar), and Craig Warner (Guitar).
