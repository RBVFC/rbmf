Luke Borchelt is a country singer, songwriter, and proud Maryland native whose music tells the story of his roots and influences. A self-taught guitarist, Luke picked up the instrument at 23 and soon began writing songs to share with those closest to him. His sound is a unique blend of classic country storytelling and the rhythms of DMV hip-hop, shaped by a diverse range of influences. Luke's music is a reflection of his upbringing in Maryland-honest, heartfelt, and always grounded in the experiences of real life. By capturing the soul of country and city, his work stands as a testament to the power of blending genres and staying true to his roots.
Wether he's touring with Shaboozy, staring at Country Calling in Ocean City, getting airtime on WPOC radio, or rocking the Commanders draft party at Northwest Stadium, Luke brings the "Crab Country" sound of his music to fans young and old.
