The Mission

Why This
Festival Matters

The Riviera Beach Music Fest is a community-driven fundraiser dedicated to supporting the Riviera Beach Volunteer Fire Company, Station {{ site.rbvfc.station }} — an all-volunteer fire, rescue, marine, and ambulance company proudly serving {{ site.rbvfc.county }} since {{ site.rbvfc.founded }}.

Every ticket purchased, every vendor booth, and every sponsor dollar goes directly toward equipment, training, and operational costs that keep our volunteer firefighters ready to protect our community around the clock.

Come for the music. Stay for the community. Give back to the heroes who give everything.

What Your Support Funds

Where The
Money Goes

🚒
Equipment

Modern firefighting and rescue equipment keeps our volunteers safe and effective on every call. Your support helps us stay up to date with tools that save lives.

🌊
Marine Rescue

As a waterfront community, marine safety is a critical part of what RBVFC does. Funds support boat maintenance, water rescue equipment, and training for marine operations.

🎓
Training

Our volunteers give their time — we give them the training they need. Funds support certification courses, drills, and continuing education so every responder is ready.

🚑
Ambulance & EMS

RBVFC provides ambulance services to our community. Your contribution helps maintain vehicles, medical supplies, and AED equipment throughout the district.

🏠
Station Operations

The firehouse is the heart of our operations. Maintenance, utilities, and daily operational costs are all covered by community fundraising — not government funding.

🤝
Community Programs

Beyond emergency response, RBVFC supports fire prevention education, community outreach, and programs that make Riviera Beach a safer place for everyone.
