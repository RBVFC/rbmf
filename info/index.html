Tickets & Entry

Tickets available online and at the gate (subject to availability). Children under 5 are free. No re-entry after exit. All sales support RBVFC Station {{ site.rbvfc.station }}.

🍺
Food & Drink

A variety of local food vendors and licensed bars on site. Outside food and beverages are not permitted. Cash and card accepted at most vendors.

🚗
Parking

Premium onsite parking available to purchase. Limited street parking for the event. Attendees are encouraged to use the free shuttle bus from local hubs. Streets around the event site will be closed to traffic.

Full parking details to be announced closer to the event.

📋
Policies

No outside alcohol. No professional cameras without press credentials. Folding chairs welcome. Pet-friendly in designated areas only. Full policy list available at the gate.
