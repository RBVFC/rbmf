Our Cause

Supporting
Those Who
Protect Us

The Riviera Beach Music Fest is a community-driven fundraiser dedicated to supporting the Riviera Beach Volunteer Fire Company, Station 13 — an all-volunteer fire, rescue, marine, and ambulance company proudly serving AA County since 1931.

Every ticket purchased, every vendor booth, and every sponsor dollar goes directly toward equipment, training, and operational costs that keep our volunteer firefighters ready to protect our community around the clock.

Come for the music. Stay for the community. Give back to the heroes who give everything.

Tickets & Entry
Tickets available online and at the gate (subject to availability). Children under 5 are free. No re-entry after exit. All sales support RBVFC #13.
🍺
Food & Drink
A variety of local food vendors and licensed bars on site. Outside food and beverages are not permitted. Cash and card accepted at most vendors.
🚗
Parking
Parking details and shuttle information will be announced closer to the event. We encourage carpooling and rideshare to minimize congestion.
📋
Policies
No outside alcohol. No professional cameras without press credentials. Folding chairs welcome. Pet-friendly in designated areas. Full policy list available at gate.
